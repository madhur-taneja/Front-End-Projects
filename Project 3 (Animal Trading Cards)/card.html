The Arctic Wolf

Arctic Wolves can live in total dark for 5 months and reach speeds of 40 mph while chasing prey.

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus arctos
  • Average Length: 3-5 Feet
  • Average Lifespan: 7 Years
  • Habitat: Alaska,Greenland and Canada

Arctic Wolves have very sharp teeth as well as powerful jaws. These wolves also have smaller ears than other species. That is part of them staying warm as well. And since the ground is frozen, they have padded paws that are designed to offer them a good grip when they walk.
